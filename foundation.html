The Phoenix Foundation

A living continuity established in memory of my mother, Samia Salim.

“And We made from water every living thing.”
— Qur’an 21:30

In Kitui, water is not infrastructure. It is survival, dignity, and future.

When Sa’d ibn ‘Ubadah lost his mother, he asked the Prophet ﷺ:
“Which charity is best on her behalf?”
The reply was: Water.

This foundation follows that path — not as symbolism, but as responsibility.
